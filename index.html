About Us

SelamBus is a modern platform that makes bus travel in Ethiopia simple and reliable. We connect travelers with trusted bus operators, offering easy search, seat selection, and secure payments. Our mission is to bring convenience and transparency to intercity travel.

Whether you’re planning a quick visit or a long journey, SelamBus helps you compare routes, reserve seats, and manage bookings — all in one place.

  • Trusted operators across major routes
  • Secure payments (TeleBirr, CBE Birr)
  • Real-time seat selection
  • Customer-first support

Help & FAQs

Use the search form to select your cities and dates, pick a bus on the results page, select seats, enter passenger details, and proceed to payment.

We support TeleBirr, CBE Birr, and bank transfer. More options will be added soon.

Cancellation and changes depend on the bus operator’s policy. Check your booking confirmation page for available options.
